Frank Lloyd Wright Projects in the United States

This MapBoxStudio design takes a look at where Frank Lloyd Wright's projects are located within the United States. The map is designed with a color scheme that gives users the feeling as if they on one of Frank Lloyd Wright's properties on a crisp autumn day, and the simplicity reflects Frank Lloyd Wright's emphasis of line and texture, in effort to make his forms more significant.
